StreamGrounds


At StreamGrounds users can download torrents and store them, so they can watch them from any device just like NETFLIX (but without content limitations ;).

User accounts are linked to my service called TheGrounds (link here) with sign ups based on a referral system.

Screenshots show a sample torrent of a public domain Charlie Chaplin movie torrent.

Currently the website deletes movies after 14 days to save space since my home server (RPi 4) is limited.

FairGrounds


FairGrounds allows users to upload and share files with just a link. I built it based on the WeTransfer concept, but WeTransfer deletes the files after a week or so, FairGrounds has no such limitation.

FairGrounds works amazingly fast for small to medium files, but due to server limitations big files may be slow.
